Common questions

Why is there a flat ₹20 for intraday?

Flat charges make trading cost predictable across order sizes and segments. This is simpler than percentage-based brokerage for active intraday traders.

Are exchange/stamp taxes included?

No. Exchange levies, STT, GST and statutory taxes are passed through and shown separately on your contract note.
