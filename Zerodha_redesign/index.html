About Us

Zerodha began its journey on August 15, 2010, with a mission to eliminate the barriers traders and investors faced in India whether in terms of cost, support, or technology. The name itself reflects this purpose: a fusion of “Zero” and “Rodha,” the Sanskrit word for barrier.

Over the years, our in-house technology and disruptive pricing models have helped us become India’s largest stock broker, with over 1.6 crore clients placing billions of orders annually and contributing more than 15% of the country’s retail trading volumes.

While we’ve come a long way, we’re constantly building, learning, and evolving whether it’s through our blog, media insights, or the philosophies that shape our products and business.
